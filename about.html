Our History

Welcome to Sri Saranapaala Pre School, a cherished hub of early childhood education that has been nurturing young minds since its establishment in 1998. With a rich history spanning over two decades, our preschool has been a beacon of learning, growth, and joy for countless children.

Our dedicated team of two experienced teachers and one temperory teacher brings a wealth of knowledge and passion to the classroom, ensuring that each child receives personalized attention and guidance. The heart of our educational philosophy lies in providing a safe, calm, and religiously inspired environment where young learners can thrive.

At Sri Saranapaala Pre School, we take pride in our friendly play area, designed to spark creativity and foster social interaction among our students. The safety of our little explorers is of paramount importance, and our play area is meticulously crafted to provide a secure space for both fun and learning. On the other hand, our teachers always monitoring kid's activities within the leisure time periods.

Embracing a variety of teaching styles, we understand that every child is unique. Our dynamic approach to education allows us to cater to diverse learning preferences, ensuring that each child finds joy and fulfillment in the learning process.

In addition to our commitment to academic excellence, we prioritize the holistic development of each child's mind. Our preschool is a hub of mind-improving activities that stimulate attitude development, encouraging creativity, and analytical thinking from an early age.

Rooted in a religious background, we aim to instill strong values and a sense of moral responsibility in our students. At Sri Saranapaala Pre School, we believe that a solid foundation in both academics and character lays the groundwork for a bright and promising future.

Come and explore the nurturing world of Sri Saranapaala Pre School, where education is not just about imparting knowledge but about fostering a love for learning that lasts a lifetime. We invite you to join our community, where young minds flourish and friendships are forged in the vibrant tapestry of early childhood education.
